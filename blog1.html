HOW INSTAGRAM THINKS

1. Understanding the Instagram Algorithm

Instagram doesn't randomly decide which posts become popular. The platform watches how people interact with your content and uses those signals to decide whether your post deserves more reach.

📱

You Post

Your content enters Instagram's system.

→
👀

People Watch

Instagram measures attention and watch time.

→
💬

People Engage

Likes, saves, comments and shares send signals.

→
🚀

More Reach

Strong signals help your content reach new people.

💜

The simple rule?

Create content that makes people stop, stay, save and share.
