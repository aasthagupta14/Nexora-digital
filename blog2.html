SEARCH INTENT DECODER

3. Keywords Are Not Enough. Understand Intent.

SEO is not only about finding keywords. It is about understanding what the person actually wants when they search. The same keyword can mean different things depending on intent.

Don't create content only around keywords. Create content around the reason behind the search.
